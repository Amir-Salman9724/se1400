Soccer Journey

Dedication on the Pitch

Soccer has been a fundamental part of my life for as long as I can remember. It has taught me the importance of discipline, perseverance, and working as part of a cohesive unit. Training sessions at Utah Tech require a high level of physical and mental focus, qualities that I translate directly into my coding projects. Just as a soccer match requires a strategic game plan, building a website requires a clear roadmap and the ability to adapt when things don't go as expected.


Teamwork and Leadership

Being an athlete means understanding that your individual performance contributes to a larger goal. On the field, I focus on communication and supporting my teammates to ensure we play at our best. This "team-first" mentality is incredibly valuable in the tech world, where software is rarely built by one person in a vacuum. I enjoy the collaborative environment of a development team, where brainstorming sessions and peer code reviews lead to better overall products.


Balancing Sports and Academics

Maintaining a high GPA while competing in sports is a challenge that has helped me master time management. Every hour is accounted for, from early morning practices to late-night coding sessions. This balance has made me a more efficient worker. I have learned how to prioritize tasks effectively, ensuring that both my athletic commitments and my educational goals are met with the same level of excellence and passion.
